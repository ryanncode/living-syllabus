## Objective

The goal of this assignment is to analyze the primary text we discussed in Module 1. You should demonstrate an understanding of the core arguments and provide your own critical perspective.

## Instructions

1. **Select a Topic:** Choose one of the three prompts listed in the assignment sheet.
2. **Length:** The essay should be 1,000 - 1,200 words.
3. **Format:** MLA or APA style, double-spaced, 12pt font.
4. **Citations:** You must cite at least three scholarly sources.

## Grading Rubric

| Criteria | Excellent (A) | Good (B) | Satisfactory (C) | Needs Improvement (D/F) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Argument** | Clear, original, and well-supported thesis. | Clear thesis with good support. | Thesis is present but weak. | No clear thesis. |
| **Analysis** | Deep engagement with the text. | Good understanding of the text. | Surface-level summary. | Misunderstanding of the text. |
| **Organization** | Logical flow and strong transitions. | Mostly organized. | Some structural issues. | Disorganized. |
| **Mechanics** | Free of grammatical errors. | Few minor errors. | Distracting errors. | Significant errors. |
