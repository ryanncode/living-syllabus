# Module 1: Introduction to the Field

---

## Overview

In this first module, we will explore the foundational concepts of our discipline. We will define key terms, look at the historical context, and identify the major questions that will guide our study throughout the course.

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this module, you will be able to:

* Define the core terminology of the field.
* Explain the historical significance of the early theories.
* Critique the initial methodologies used by researchers.
