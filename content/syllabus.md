## Course Description
This course explores the intersection of digital technology and humanities research. We will examine how digital tools are reshaping the way we study literature, history, and culture.

## Learning Objectives
By the end of this course, students will be able to:
1. Understand the history and scope of Digital Humanities.
2. Critically evaluate digital tools and methods.
3. Create a digital project using standard technologies.

## Required Materials
* *Debates in the Digital Humanities*, edited by Matthew K. Gold.
* All other readings will be provided as PDFs on Canvas.
